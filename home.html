Advantages of whole genome
sequencing for your IVF cycle

Embryo genome screening coverage

SNPs, insertions & deletions, copy number variations, mosaicism and chromosome aneuploidies

Parent to child screening﻿

We screen transmitted variants from the embryos parents with an accuracy of >99.9%

De novo
mutations

New mutations introduced at fertilisation are detected and graded for screening (>95.1% accuracy)

Step 1

Whole-genome screening with GenomeScreen™ in the GenEmbryomics patient portal will allow you to provide your doctors information who can arrange for DNA sample collection.

Step 2

The embryos genome sequence is analysed to determine if they carry any damaging mutations that may be threatening their health.

Step 3

The GenomeScreen™ report is a summary of all the genetic data related to disease, and it offers guidance for the future health of your embryos.
